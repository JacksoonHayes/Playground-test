Decision Support System
What the system does/is A DSS is a computer program that analyses large amounts of data to support business decisions.
Its place in the business world/type(s) of decisions being made DSS's would be used by planning departments in organisations to provide useful decision-making information to managers. The data that is processed through the DSS model is used to help management departments to educated guesses about the company's future relating to profits, this can be useful because projected profits determine how a company may run.
Types of data input Data can come from internal and external sources meaning the types of data inputs can be relational data sources, multi-dimensional arrays, EDW's (data warehouses. Sales figures between one sales period and another, and projected revenue/ and or sales figures.
Type of people who use it. HR and/or higher ups
Examples Agricultural production. Forestry management from log transportation, harvest scheduling, sustainability and ecosystems.
